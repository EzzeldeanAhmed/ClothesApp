Our Story

Not Chilly started with a single vision: to make streetwear hoodies that keep you warm, stylish, and confident. Born in Cairo, our brand combines bold design with everyday comfort.

🔥 Our Mission

We believe in more than just fashion. Our mission is to craft hoodies that empower individuals to express themselves while staying true to ethical and sustainable values.

🌱 Sustainable Materials

Each hoodie is made from eco-friendly cotton blends and durable fabrics. By sourcing responsibly, we reduce waste and minimize our environmental impact.

🤝 Local Craftsmanship

Every piece is designed and produced locally in Egypt, supporting artisans and strengthening our community. When you wear Not Chilly, you wear a story stitched with care.

💡 Join the Movement

Not Chilly isn’t just a brand — it’s a culture of boldness, warmth, and authenticity. Wherever you go, our hoodies keep your style hot.
